Why does this website look so simple?

I'm a big fan of Motherfucking Website and its counterpart Better Motherfucking Website. I think that the web should be designed around simple, handmade things and believe that I can be a part of that.

This website uses MathJax and Highlight.js to render math and code; however, I intend for this site to be readable even without them enabled. Feel free to disable scripts if you like.
